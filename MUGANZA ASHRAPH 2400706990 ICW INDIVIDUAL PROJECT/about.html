WHAT IS GREEN ENERGY?

Green energy refers to energy that is generated from natural, renewable resources that have a minimal impact on the environment. This includes sources such as solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal energy. Green energy is essential for reducing our reliance on fossil fuels and combating climate change.
Unlike fossil fuels like coal and oil, which release harmful pollutants and greenhouse gases when burned, green energy sources are clean and sustainable.
